How to Play Android Games in PC without using Emulator.

  • By Deep
  • May 13, 2021
  • PC, Gaming

You Know that for Playing Android Games in PC we have to use Emulator. But after reading this Post you can really play Android Games in your Pc without Emulator.

Read the Steps Carefully

Installation

Step 1: Download TC Games Software from the given link http://www.sigma-rt.com/en/tcgames/

Step 2: Then Install it Properly.

Step 3: Then Install TC Games in Your Android Mobile From Play Store

Connecting Process

Step 1: In Mobile Phone Open Mobile Settings and Click on System.

Step 2: Then Click on Developer Options.

Step 3: Then Enable Developer Options.

Step 4: In Developer Options Find USB Dubugging and Enable it.

Step 5: Then Connect USB Wire with Pc.

Step 6: In Pc Open TC Games and Click on the Connect Botton given on the Top.

Step 7: Then Click on Agree

Now your Mobile Phone is Connected with your Pc.

How To Play Game

Step 1: Open Any Game in Your Mobile.

Step 2: After Opening Game in Mobile. In Your PC TC Games Automatically set the Key Framing and Now You can Play Game Easily
